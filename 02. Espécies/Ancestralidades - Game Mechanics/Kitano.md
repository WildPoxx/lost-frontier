# Kitano

Kitanos are sterile human-Kadiff hybrids, often underestimated by formal institutions but highly valued in Rust Hollow’s Second Trail Program. Their cognition is poorly suited to abstract formal systems, but they possess heightened sensory perception, strong instinctive reading of animals, and unusual resonance with Bondaks and Tal’Bondaks.

## Ancestry Traits

### Adaptive Hybrid Build
Choose two different attributes from Agility, Spirit, Strength, or Vigor. Each chosen attribute starts at d6. Smarts cannot be selected.

### Acute Senses
Kitanos gain +2 to Notice rolls involving smell, close hearing, spoor, animal behavior, chemical changes, residue, fungus trails, warm metal, or environmental shifts.

### Bondak Intuition
Kitanos gain +1 to Survival, Notice, or handling-related rolls involving Bondaks, Tal’Bondaks, spoor interpretation, animal hesitation, feeding behavior, stress signs, or energy-residue trails.

### Latent Resonance
Kitanos can form unusually strong emotional and sensory bonds with Bondaks and Tal’Bondaks. With a bonded animal, they may sense strong emotional states, distress, fear, hunger, pain, direction of attention, or unusual fixation at the GM’s discretion. This does not allow mind reading, remote control, sensory possession, or shared Wounds unless expanded by specific Kitano Edges.

### Compact Frame
Size -1.

### Clueless
Kitanos have the Clueless Hindrance.

### All Thumbs
Kitanos have the All Thumbs Hindrance.

### Illiterate
Kitanos have the Illiterate Hindrance.

### Outsider
Kitanos have Outsider (Minor).

### Sterile
Kitanos are sterile.
