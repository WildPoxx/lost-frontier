# 1. Arte Conceitual Geral — Abutres de Aço

## Descrição

Imagem de grupo da facção inteira. Deve apresentar os Abutres de Aço como uma gangue de saqueadores de fronteira, predadores de sucata e ladrões de prospecção. Eles não devem parecer heróis, soldados profissionais ou personagens estilizados demais. A composição deve mostrar variedade interna: recrutas nervosos, catadores experientes, batedores, sabotadores, brutamontes e líderes de bando.

A imagem deve transmitir que eles vivem de esperar outros prospectores cansarem, se ferirem ou perderem equipamento para então atacar. Devem parecer sujos, práticos, improvisados e perigosos em bando. O símbolo da facção deve aparecer como uma asa grosseira pintada, riscada ou soldada em placas, veículos e armaduras — não como adereço tribal exagerado.

## Prompt

```text
Create a 3:4 western semi-realistic RPG sourcebook illustration of the Steel Vultures, a loose gang of outlaw scavengers and salvage raiders from the Rust Hollow frontier.

They are not soldiers, not heroes, and not stylish anime raiders. They should look like desperate, predatory frontier criminals: dirty, tired, practical, dangerous in a cowardly pack. Their gear is improvised from salvage: patched dust coats, torn scarves, cracked work goggles, welding masks, battered helmets, mismatched scrap armor, old cargo plates, rope, hooks, cutting tools, cheap firearms, sawed-off shotguns and stolen salvage bags.

The group stands in a dusty wreck field near the Campo das Estrelas Cadentes, surrounded by broken satellites, rusted machinery, metallic sand, old vehicle parts and distant industrial silhouettes. A few jury-rigged bikes and battered buggies sit nearby, marked with a crude wing symbol painted in rust-red and black.

Their faces and body language should feel grounded and western: weathered, suspicious, hungry, cruel, exhausted, not glamorous. Some look young and frightened, some look old and mean, some hide behind goggles or dust masks. The composition should feel like a gritty tabletop RPG faction illustration or a western graphic novel splash page, cinematic but restrained.

Style: western semi-realistic RPG sourcebook illustration, grounded sci-fi western, gritty frontier realism, American and European graphic novel influence, realistic human proportions, weathered faces, practical salvage gear, worn fabrics, battered metal, dust, rust, oil stains, muted painterly rendering, serious frontier atmosphere, red storm sky, no glossy 3D render.
```

## Prompt negativo

# 2. Abutre Raspa

## Descrição

O **Abutre Raspa** é o membro mais baixo da hierarquia. É recruta, bucha de canhão e capanga descartável. Deve parecer jovem, subnutrido, nervoso e mal equipado. Ele tenta parecer perigoso, mas sua postura entrega insegurança. É alguém que entrou no bando por fome, dívida, medo ou falta de alternativa.

O equipamento deve ser ruim: pistola velha, faca enferrujada, colete de sucata mal ajustado, óculos rachados, saco de carga grande demais. Ele deve parecer mais trágico e patético do que estiloso. Ainda assim, em bando, pode ser cruel.

## Prompt

```text
Create a 3:4 western semi-realistic RPG sourcebook illustration of an Abutre Raspa, the lowest-ranking member of the Steel Vultures.

He is a young, nervous, underfed salvage raider from the Rust Hollow frontier, trying to look dangerous but clearly afraid when alone. His clothing is practical and worn: torn frontier pants, patched dust shirt, cracked work goggles, dirty scarf over the mouth and a badly fitted scrap vest made from thin mismatched metal plates. He carries a rusty knife, a small unreliable junk pistol, wire cutters and an oversized salvage sack patched with scratched-out company markings.

His posture should show false bravado: shoulders tense, eyes alert, one hand close to the pistol but not confident. Add a crude wing symbol painted badly on his chest plate or salvage sack. Background: rusted barrels, broken machinery, metallic dust and distant vehicle lights.

Style: western semi-realistic RPG sourcebook illustration, grounded sci-fi western, gritty frontier realism, realistic human proportions, weathered face, practical salvage clothing, worn fabrics, battered metal, rust, dust, muted painterly rendering, no glossy 3D render.
```

## Prompt negativo

```text
No anime style, no manga style, no heroic pose, no clean armor, no advanced military gear, no polished weapons, no muscular superhero body, no fantasy rogue outfit, no cartoon proportions, no cute face, no glossy 3D render, no neon cyberpunk background.
```

# 3. Abutre Catador

## Descrição

O **Abutre Catador** é o membro padrão da facção. Ele já sobreviveu tempo suficiente para saber emboscar, roubar, fugir e vender peças. Não é elite, mas é funcional. Deve parecer mais confiante que o Raspa: magro, sujo, alerta, oportunista e prático.

Visualmente, é o saqueador comum: casaco remendado, lenço de poeira, óculos de solda, colete de metal reaproveitado, ganchos, cordas, lâmina curta, pistola leve e escopeta serrada. Deve parecer alguém que prefere roubar uma equipe cansada a lutar de frente.

## Prompt

```text
Create a 3:4 western semi-realistic RPG sourcebook illustration of an Abutre Catador, a standard Steel Vultures scavenger-raider.

He is lean, dirty, alert and practical, an experienced outlaw scavenger who survives by robbing exhausted prospectors near Rust Hollow. He wears a patched long coat over a scrap vest, a dust scarf, cracked welding goggles pushed up over his forehead, mismatched metal plates from old cargo panels and several stolen salvage tags hanging from his belt. He carries salvage hooks, rope, a short serrated blade, a light pistol and a sawed-off shotgun.

His expression should be predatory and opportunistic, not heroic. He looks like someone who waits for others to do the hard work before stealing the result. Behind him, include a rusty light bike or small buggy with a crude wing symbol scratched into the side.

Style: western semi-realistic RPG sourcebook illustration, grounded sci-fi western, American and European graphic novel influence, realistic human proportions, weathered face, practical salvage gear, worn fabric, battered metal, rust, dust, oil stains, muted painterly rendering, no glossy 3D render.
```

## Prompt negativo

No anime style, no manga style, no clean mercenary armor, no tactical military uniform, no futuristic elite soldier look, no pristine shotgun, no heroic western cowboy glamour, no cartoon style, no glossy 3D render, no neon city, no symmetrical uniform.

# 4. Abutre Batedor

Deve transmitir paciência predatória: alguém que se esconde entre sucata, poeira e ruínas, esperando a chance certa.

O visual deve ser discreto e funcional: capa de poeira, capuz, rifle leve, binóculo ou rangefinder analógico, sinalizadores e moto leve camuflada com poeira e sucata.

He wears a long dust cloak with a hood, cracked field goggles, a dirty scarf, battered light armor under the cloak and practical salvage boots. He carries a light rifle, an analog rangefinder, signal flares, scrap markers and a compact scrambler beacon used to call ambushes.

Show him crouched behind a broken satellite panel or rusted vehicle hull, observing distant prey. A light off-road bike is parked nearby, covered in dust and improvised camouflage. He should feel like a scavenger-hunter who marks weak targets for the pack, not like a military sniper.

# 5. Abutre Quebra-Marca

Seu perigo não está em matar, mas em adulterar selos, sabotar veículos, apagar marcas de propriedade e transformar carga roubada em mercadoria vendável.

# Nota final de uso no DALL·E

Para as imagens individuais, recomendo **não insistir muito em máscaras exageradas ou penas metálicas**. O símbolo dos Abutres deve aparecer principalmente como:

```text
a crude wing symbol painted, scratched or welded onto armor plates, bags or vehicle parts
```

Evite repetir demais:

```text
metal feathers on shoulders and helmets
```

Isso reduz a chance de o gerador puxar para estética tribal/anime pós-apocalíptica.
